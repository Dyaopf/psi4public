
.. glossary::

   SAPT_OS_SCALE
      The SAPT module will print a decomposition of the :math:`E_{disp}^{(20)}`
      and :math:`E_{exch-disp}^{(20)}` terms into same-spin and opposite-spin
      contributions, in analogy to the SCS-MP2 method of Stefan Grimme. This
      option controls the scaling of the opposite-spin contributions.
    
      * **Type**: double
      * **Default**: 6/5
