Interfaces: Enhancing PSI4 Capabilities
=======================================

|PSIfour|'s capabilites have been expanded by interfacing with other
software packages. These programs aren't necessary 
for |PSIfour| to operate and may require additional licences, special 
platform capabilities, *etc*. 

* :ref:`sec:mrcc` PSI4 can drive the MRCC program of M. Kallay and J. Gauss
* MPI4Py
* MADNESS
